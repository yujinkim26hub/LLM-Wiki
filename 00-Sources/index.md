# Sources

The **raw inputs** layer of the wiki — immutable papers, articles, books, images and data. Nothing here is edited; the wiki reads these and distils them into [[10-Summaries/index|Summaries]], then weaves them into [[30-Concepts/index|Concepts]], [[40-Topics/index|Topics]] and [[50-Notes/index|Notes]].

Open-access source files (e.g. papers) are published in full so you can browse straight to the original alongside its summary.

## Collections

- [[00-Sources/papers/index|Papers]] — academic papers (incl. [[00-Sources/papers/virtual/index|virtual perturbation]])
- [[00-Sources/articles/index|Articles]] — web articles and essays
- [[00-Sources/books/index|Books]] — book-length sources
- [[00-Sources/images/index|Images]] — figures and image inputs
